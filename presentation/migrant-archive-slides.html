Architecture Decision

Two transcription strategies.
One shared contract.

VideoData dataclass — the pipeline never knows which strategy ran.

Strategy A

A

YouTube Captions

$0 cost Instant No punctuation

Free and fast. Used as fallback only.

Strategy B

B

WhisperX + Speaker Diarisation

$0 cost High quality Speaker labels

Same Whisper model + alignment + pyannote. Multi-speaker ready.

The Difference

+22% more text extracted
✓ proper punctuation
0 garbled segments

Whisper output is directly usable for RAG. Captions need heavy post-processing.
